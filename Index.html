Things you should know

Looking forward for employment in UAE? There are a few things you need to know before
you come to Dubai. Dubai submerged in centuries of tradition and pious religious following.
To ensure that your stay is lasting, respect the laws and values of Dubai. Here are some
points you should give a thought on:

Things You should Do:

•  Respect Religion

Moral Islamic Principles are highly esteemed in the Dubai. Display of any contempt towards
Islamic beliefs or practices is considered deeply derogatory which may result you in a
problem. Following other religions by the expatriate community is accepted.

•  Dress-Up Modestly

Emirati’s dress traditionally. Dressing inappropriately or not in accordance with Islamic values
is considered to be an offense. You should avoid clothes that are transparent or which
indecently exposes body parts, offensive pictures, slogans in communal places such as
shopping malls, eatery and public parks.


Things you should know

•  Adapt to the Culture and Traditions

Dubai stands on strong backing of Islamic traditions which are deeply rooted in its cultural
heritage. This heritage has been worn by generation to generation forming the very bedrock
of everyday life. You can have an open minded approach but respect the culture and values
of Dubai.

•  Experience the Local Cuisine and Cultural Heritage

Dubai is also cradle to various heritage sites that enlightens about the colorful past of the
city. Dubai’s cuisine mirrors the food habits of the Middle East. The primary cuisine preferred
by the people here is Arabian , Iranian, Moroccan and Lebanese foods.


Things you should know

Things You shouldn’t Do:

×     Don’t Possess Illegal Drugs

Dealing narcotics is considered a serious transgression which can result in life-incarceration.
Dubai has very strict policy code for drug/medication. Medicines especially those with
codeine are impermissible. Even for personal use a specific doctor’s prescription is required

×     Don’t Drink and Drive     

Dubai has a zero-tolerance code for drinking and driving. You can be Imposed
and incarcerated, if seized with even minute traces of alcohol in your system.

×    Don’t Buy Alcohol without License

Alcohol Consumption is permitted only to Non-Muslims in licensed restaurants and bars.
Alcohol in these stores can only be purchased for consumption at home by liquor license
residents. This license does not give any dispensation for alcohol related illegal
infringement. The license is permitted to people above the age of 21.


Things you should know

×     Don’t Take photos without permission

Avoid taking of photographs of people, secure areas (airport, military camps, official
buildings, etc.) .

×     Don’t Behave Inappropriate     
The civilization and lifestyle of Dubai is sketched to safeguard that everyone respects each
other regardless of their belief and ethnic culture. You should keep yourself away from
improper conduct and behavior which can lead you to fines, imprisonment and deportation.

Repatriation Ticket:

Recruits:

All expatriate employees (other than that are already resident) are entitled to one free firm
one way economy joining ticket. The journey will normally be from the Annual Leave
Destination given in the New Starters Form. Any exceptions must be approved by HR
following written request. If for Visa transfer purposes it is necessary to leave/re-enter Dubai,

joining ticket for the 4th child at the employee’s expense. The employee will incur and be
responsible for the ‘Visa Transfer’ ticket plus costs incurred for visit visas.

Telephone


Getting a telephone in Dubai is quite painless. There are two telecom providers in Dubai
Etisalat and Du. Each provides good service, excellent network coverage, reasonable calling
rates and variety of plans to choose from.

In order to get a fixed phone line, you need to visit any of the operator’s Service/Business
Centre. Bring along following documents:

•  Your original passport and copies.
•  Your residency visa copies.
•  Your tenancy agreement.
•  Letter from us stating your income.

A telephone line is usually installed within one or two working days at most. You can also call
800101 for Etisalat Customer Care Center and 800155 for Du. Calls from one fixed line to
another within the UAE are free.

Television and Internet

           How to bring your family to
       Dubai?-




Your Spouse and Children

Before Arrival:

There are various steps to successfully sponsor your family in UAE.

We would assist you in bringing your family. In fact, the hassle of going to the Immigration
Department is avoided.

You just need to submit the required documents to Employee Service Centre.

Following are the documents you need to submit for residence visa of your family:

•Copy of your passport with visa page.
•Copy of your family member’s passport.
•Two passport sized photographs of your family member with white background.
•Original Marriage Certificate, must be attested Ministry of Foreign Affairs in your
  country, the UAE Embassy of your country and by the Ministry of Foreign Affairs in
  Dubai
•Children’s birth certificates (the original ones /the attested copies)
•Salary certificate in Arabic (prepared by flydubai).


Your Spouse and Children

Process:
• Take the above documentation to any typing center to type a Residence Visa application
   form and take the form, along with the mentioned documents, to our immigration office at
   HQ. Immigration will issue a temporary A4 pink residence visa.

• You need to deposit the temporary A4 at the terminal of their arrival and send a copy to
   your family

• The copy should be presented by your family at the visa collection point, located before
   passport control. The original temporary A4 pink residence visa will be given in return for
   the copy.

• If your family member is currently inside the country, Firstly, you can return to the typing
  center with your visit visa and type a Change Status application form. This will allow your
  family member to change their visa status from Visit to Resident without leaving the
  country.Once the form is typed, return to immigration with your family member’s original
  passport to complete the process.

• Secondly, Exit the country and return on the visa.



Your Spouse and Children

After Arrival:

•  Once your family arrives the medical exam for your wife and children above 17 should be
   conducted at the Medical Centers and you will get the result within 3 working days.

•  You need to apply for Emirates ID and stamp the residence visa on your wife and
    children’s passport after you get the medical test results.

•  Return to the typing Centre and type a Residence Visa Stamping application form in
   Arabic. Take the form, along with the necessary documentation, to the immigration office
   at HQ.

•  The following are the documents you need to submit to Employee Service Centre-
•  Original passport, Original Visas of your family.
•  Your passport copy (sponsor / husband).
•  Two (2) pcs. passport size photos with white background for each person
•  Medical reports.

The passports with residence visa stamped will be available after 1-2 weeks and 2-3
days for urgent basis. It will be valid for 3 years.

Your Parents and In-laws


Expatriates can sponsor their parents and In-laws in Dubai with one year renewable resident
visas for their parents or parents-in-law. Rules for the same are quite different from that of
sponsoring your spouse and children.

Following criteria is to be considered for the eligibility and of sponsoring your parents
and In-laws:


•  Valid resident visas with a minimum salary of AED 20,000 with accommodation of
   minimum 2 bedrooms.

•  If your salary is lower than this, you can seek the Humanitarian Desk at DNRD, who deal
   with individual cases on a humanitarian basis.

•  You have to sponsor both your parents together with proof of you being the sole provider
   to them. In case, your parents are divorced or one is deceased, you should carry a valid
    proof, when visiting DNRD to obtain the entry permit visa.

•  You also need to obtain a minimum medical insurance coverage of AED 600 per year for
   each parent.


Your Parents and In-laws


Documents required for entry visa:

•  Application form typed in Arabic.
•  Your Original passport.
•  Passport copy of Parents/In-laws
•  1 Passport sized photo in white background.
•  Attested proof of relationship from your country embassy/consulate stating both your
    relationship and that you are sole provider for your parent/In-laws.
•  Salary Certificate from flydubai.


Procedure:

•  Visit General Directorate of Residency and Foreigners Affairs Dubai with all valid
   documents and a letter appealing on humanitarian grounds for entry visa for your parents
   and In-laws. Your application will be either approved or rejected within two weeks.

•  If Approved, complete the form from a typing centre after paying the fees and submit the
   documents to DNRD.

•  Entry Permit will be sent by post within 48 hours.


Your Parents and In-laws

The entry visa should be converted to a residency within 60 days from the date of entry. You
need following documents:


•  Application form typed in Arabic.
•  3 passport sized photographs with white background.
•  Your Original passport .
•  Original entry visa.
•  Medical insurance policy of your parents/In-laws
•  Refundable deposit receipt.
•  Salary certificate given by flydubai.


Fees:

•   A refundable deposit of AED 2000 which will be reimbursed when the visa is cancelled or
   your parents dies on showing the receipt. This deposit receipt is also utilized during visa

•  Application form fee of AED 110.

How to get a Driving License


UAE citizen/residents can attain a driving license only after training from an authorized
driving institute in Dubai. In case you are 18-21 year of age, you can apply for a probationary
license.

New Drivers:

If you do not possess any driving license then you will have to undergo 40 classes.


Drivers with foreign driving license:

If you are possessing your country's license which is about 2 – 5 years old then you are
eligible to take 30 classes and country’s license which is above 5 years are eligible to take 20
classes.


Transfer of existing driving license:

You could be eligible to transfer your existing foreign driving license to UAE driving license
without even going through any tests or going to driving school. Check RTA’s website
www.rta.ae to know which countries license can be transferred.


How to get a Driving License


Documents required:

•  Passport ( original & copy) with visa page.
•  NOC from flydubai.
•  8 Passport size photos.


Steps:

•  Get an eye test done either at the driving school or at any optical center.

•  After submitting your documents, the school will take care of the paper works.

•  You will receive a temporary driving license and you must carry that during your
    training session.

•  Once you have passed all the test like garage parking, parallel parking and then the
    school will register you for theory test, followed by road test.

•  For further information you wish for, you could contact RTA directly – 800 90 90.

How to get your Emirates ID
  • Emirates ID card (also called the National Identity Card) is an identification card, issued
    by the Emirates Identity Authority (EIA) (EIDA), that all UAE citizens (optional for
    diplomats) and residents are required to obtain.
  • It will be necessary to produce your Emirates ID card to use UAE government services,
    and sometimes other services.
  • It is mandatory for all citizens and expatriate residents of the UAE to have an Emirates ID
    card. Emirates ID card is an electronic identification card issued by the EIDA
  • It contains an electronic chip that stores vital information about the cardholder such as
    name, date of birth, nationality, marital status, photo, fingerprint data, electronic signature.
  • Soon, the EIDA would make the Emirates ID card mandatory to access government
    services. Already, several government departments have made the requirement of
    Emirates ID card mandatory for availing their services.

How to apply for Housing
Advance Allowance

  • The tenancy contract must be in the employees name and signed by both the employee
    and the landlord.
  • Payments of Security Deposit and Commission can be included within the initial advance
    if stated on the tenancy contract. Please ensure that all documentation is readable.
  • For new employees and new tenancy contract holders a cheque can be issued after three
    working days of submission of the application.
  • You will receive an e-mail notification advising you to collect your cheque from the Payroll
    Department.
  • The cheque will be payable to the employee and can be either deposited into the
    employees bank account or taken as cash from any Emirates National Bank of Dubai
    (ENBD) branch.

How to apply for Housing
Advance Allowance

  • All other HAA will be paid through the monthly payroll. Please plan ahead and submit any
    request using the following timeframes.
•  If your rental cheque is due between the 1st - 26th of the month, the request must
   be submitted by the 15th of the previous month.
•  If your rental cheque is due between the 27th - 31st of the month, the request must
    be submitted by the 15th of the same month.

    submits an application knowing it to be untrue will be liable to disciplinary action.

How to get Pension (for UAE and
GCC nationals)

Pension schemes are only available for UAE and GCC nationals as per UAE and Individual
GCC countries Labor law.

Medical is required to obtain a medical certificate for the Pension Scheme-

You will need to:
•  Book an appointment for a Pre-Employment medical in Dubai Hospital, Al Baraha
   Hospital in Dubai or Unicare Hospital in Dubai or Unicare Hospital.
•  Make sure you are fasting for a minimum of 12 hours before going for the medical
   test.
•  You will get your medical certificate in 2 working days.

The above expenses related to the medical test will be reimbursed by us.

How to register your family and
friends for travel benefits

  • You, your spouse and 3 children below 19 years of age are eligible for travel benefits with
    flydubai from the date of joining.
  • After 6 months of service you, your spouse and 3 children are eligible for travel benefits
    by Emirates and Air Arabia.
  • With flydubai you can now even register your five friends or other family members who
    can also use these special fares.
  • You just have to notify stafftravel@flydubai.com with a mail of your family (spouse and 3
    children below 19 years of age) and of your five nominations along with their name,
    nationality & passport details. Choose your nominated friends carefully as you can only
    change them once a year - in February only.
  • If you have any questions or concerns, please contact the flydubai staff travel team
    (stafftravel@flydubai.com ).
